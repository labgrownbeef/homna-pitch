# 7.2 Alerting and Notification

* **Alert Levels:** Critical, High, Medium, Low with defined response procedures
* **Alert Channels:** Console, SMS, email, and messaging platforms
* **Auto-Remediation:** Self-healing procedures for known issues
* **Escalation:** Automatic escalation paths for unresolved issues
