# 7.1 Performance Monitoring

* **Latency Measurement:**
  * Hardware timestamping at network interfaces
  * Software instrumentation at key processing points
  * End-to-end latency tracking for market data and orders
* **System Metrics:**
  * CPU, memory, disk, and network utilization
  * Queue depths and processing rates
  * Thread scheduling and context switching stats
* **Trading Metrics:**
  * Order rates, fill rates, and rejection rates
  * Strategy performance and P\&L
  * Position and exposure metrics
