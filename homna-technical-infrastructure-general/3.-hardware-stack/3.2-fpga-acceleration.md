# 3.2 FPGA Acceleration

* **Hardware:** Xilinx Alveo U250 and Intel Stratix 10 MX FPGA cards
* **Function:** Market data normalization, order book reconstruction, and basic trading signal generation
* **Latency Performance:** Sub-microsecond processing time (typically 300-800 nanoseconds)
* **Implementation Approach:**
  * Direct exchange protocol handlers in FPGA fabric
  * Hardware-accelerated TCP/IP and UDP stacks
  * Hardware-based risk checks for pre-trade validation
