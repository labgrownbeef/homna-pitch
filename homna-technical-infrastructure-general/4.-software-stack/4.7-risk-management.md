# 4.7 Risk Management

* **Pre-Trade Risk Checks:**
  * Position limits by instrument and portfolio
  * Order size and value limits
  * Price tolerance checks
  * Duplicate order detection
* **Post-Trade Monitoring:**
  * Real-time P\&L calculation
  * Risk factor exposure tracking
  * VaR and stress testing
* **Circuit Breakers:** Automated trading halts based on P\&L, volume, or error rates
