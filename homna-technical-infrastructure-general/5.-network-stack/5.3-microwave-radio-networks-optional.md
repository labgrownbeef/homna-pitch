# 5.3 Microwave/Radio Networks (Optional)

* **Technology Selection:**
  * Traditional microwave (6-23 GHz) for longer distances
  * Millimeter wave (60-80 GHz) for shorter, high-capacity links
* **Key Routes:**
  * Riyadh to Dubai: \~850 km, multi-hop configuration
  * Dubai to Abu Dhabi: \~150 km, direct link
* **Latency Advantage:** 30-40% improvement over fiber connections
* **Redundancy:** Automatic failover to fiber routes during adverse weather
