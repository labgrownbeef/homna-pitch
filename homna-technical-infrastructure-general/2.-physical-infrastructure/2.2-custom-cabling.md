# 2.2 Custom Cabling

**2.2.1 Fiber Optic Infrastructure**

Standard fiber optic cables consist of a glass core surrounded by cladding that reflects light back into the core, enabling high-speed data transmission over long distances.

* **Single-Mode Fiber:** Used for longer distances with 9μm core diameter
* **Multi-Mode Fiber:** Used for shorter distances with 50μm or 62.5μm core diameter
* **Cable Category:** OM4 and OS2 standards
* **Connectors:** LC and MPO/MTP connectors

**2.2.2 Hollow Core Fiber (HCF)**

For critical market data paths, we employ hollow core fiber which uses air as the transmission medium instead of glass, significantly reducing latency.

* **Latency Improvement:** \~30-40% faster signal propagation compared to conventional fiber
* **Implementation:** Direct connections between market data gateways and trading engines
* **Technology:** 19-cell NANF (Nested Antiresonant Nodeless Fiber) design
* **Application:** Primary market data paths and critical order routing
