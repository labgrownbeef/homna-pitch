# 6.2 Failover and Redundancy

* **Component Redundancy:** Critical systems deployed in active-active or active-passive pairs
* **Network Redundancy:** Multiple physical paths between key locations
* **Data Center Redundancy:** Ability to operate from secondary facilities
* **Automated Failover:** Self-healing capabilities with minimal human intervention
* **Recovery Time Objectives:**
  * Critical trading functions: : < 90 to 120 seconds
  * Market data processing: < 30 to 60 seconds
  * Analytical functions: <5 minutes
