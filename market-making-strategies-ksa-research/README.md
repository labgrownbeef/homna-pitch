**Introduction**

This technical documentation provides a comprehensive overview of HOMNA's market making strategies specifically adapted for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) markets. Our proprietary approaches are designed to capture the unique characteristics and inefficiencies of the Saudi market while delivering superior risk-adjusted returns--_**most directional risk is completely hedged in our market making strategies.**_&#x20;

Market making is a critical strategy within HOMNA's portfolio, allocated 35% of trading capital due to its exceptional return profile (30-85% annually) and the advantageous spread characteristics of the Saudi market (0.5-3% vs. 0.05-0.1% in developed markets).



#### Key Advantages in Saudi Markets

* **Wider Spreads:** Saudi stocks exhibit bid-ask spreads 5-30x wider than developed markets, creating substantial profit opportunities
* **Lower Competition:** Limited algorithmic trading competition compared to developed markets
* **Favorable Market Structure:** Tadawul exchange rules and microstructure create specific opportunities for sophisticated market makers
* **Retail Dominance:** High retail participation (over 67% of daily volume) creates predictable order flow patterns
* **Evolving Market:** Ongoing market structure changes under Vision 2030 create transitional inefficiencies
