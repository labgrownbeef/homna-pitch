# 3.4.2 Saudi-Specific Model Features

**Prayer Time Effects:** Modeling liquidity shifts around daily prayer times

* **Retail Flow Patterns:** Capturing predictable retail trader behaviors in Saudi markets
* **Sectoral Dependencies:** Stronger intra-sector correlations in Saudi stocks
* **Ramadan Effect:** Adjusted models for trading behavior during Ramadan
* **Government Announcement Timing:** Models for price action around scheduled government announcements
