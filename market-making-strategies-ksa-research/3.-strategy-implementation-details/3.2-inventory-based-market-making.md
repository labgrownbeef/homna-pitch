# 3.2 Inventory-Based Market Making

**Risk Management Parameters**

| Parameter                   | Saudi Mid-Cap | Saudi Large-Cap | Saudi MSCI Index |
| --------------------------- | ------------- | --------------- | ---------------- |
| Max Inventory (% of ADV)    | 0.8%          | 1.2%            | 1.5%             |
| Inventory Half-Life         | 60 mins       | 45 mins         | 30 mins          |
| Skew Parameter (λ)          | 0.85          | 0.75            | 0.65             |
| Vol Scaling Factor          | 2.5           | 2.0             | 1.8              |
| Max Position (% of capital) | 4%            | 7%              | 10%              |

Our inventory-based model uses a mean-reversion assumption with a half-life parameter calibrated specifically for each Saudi stock based on its historical price behavior. This approach allows for dynamic inventory management while capitalizing on the wider spreads available in the Saudi market.
