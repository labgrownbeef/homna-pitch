# 3.1.2 Saudi-Specific Adaptations

* **Spread Targeting:** Target spreads 2-3x wider than global markets due to Saudi market characteristics
* **Quote Timing:** Strategic quote placement during lower volatility periods (10:30-11:30 AST and 13:00-14:00 AST)
* **Order Expiration:** Longer quote persistence due to the Saudi market's lower order cancellation rates
* **Queue Position:** Enhanced emphasis on obtaining favorable queue positions at key price levels
