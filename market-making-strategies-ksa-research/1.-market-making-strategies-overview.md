# 1. Market Making Strategies Overview

| Strategy                      | Description                                                          | Expected Return | Optimal Market Conditions                       | Risk Profile |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| Passive Market Making         | Post limit orders on both sides of the order book to capture spreads | 30-45%          | Lower volatility, consistent volume             | Low          |
| Inventory-Based Market Making | Dynamically adjust quotes based on inventory position                | 40-65%          | Medium volatility, trending markets             | Medium       |
| Aggressive Market Making      | Actively cross spreads to capture short-term alpha                   | 50-85%          | High volatility, news-driven markets            | High         |
| Statistical Market Making     | Leverage statistical patterns to predict order flow                  | 45-75%          | Any market conditions with predictable patterns | Medium-High  |
| Combined Approach             | Dynamic allocation across strategies based on market conditions      | 57% (average)   | All market conditions                           | Medium       |
