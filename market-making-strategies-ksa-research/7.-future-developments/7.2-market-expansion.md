# 7.2 Market Expansion

* **Dubai Financial Market (DFM):** Targeted launch in Q3 2025
* **Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange (ADX):** Targeted launch in Q4 2025
* **Qatar Stock Exchange (QE):** Planned for Q1 2026
* **Kuwait Boursa:** Planned for Q2 2026

#### Vision 2030 Alignment

Our market making strategies align with Saudi Vision 2030 objectives by:

* Enhancing market liquidity and efficiency
* Reducing transaction costs for all market participants
* Supporting the development of Saudi Arabia's capital markets
* Contributing to the technological advancement of Saudi financial infrastructure
* Creating high-skilled employment opportunities in financial technology
