# 2. Saudi Market Structure Analysis

| Parameter              | Tadawul Value                  | Developed Market Comparison         | Market Making Implication                                |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Average Bid-Ask Spread | 0.5-3.0%                       | 0.05-0.1%                           | Significantly higher spread capture opportunity          |
| Tick Size              | Tiered (0.1-0.5 SAR)           | Smaller, uniform ticks              | Strategic quote placement at tier boundaries             |
| Trading Hours          | 10:00-15:00 AST                | Longer trading sessions             | Concentrated liquidity and distinctive intraday patterns |
| Circuit Breakers       | ±10% daily limits              | More flexible limits                | Risk management around limit scenarios                   |
| Settlement Cycle       | T+2                            | T+1 or T+2                          | Standard settlement risk management                      |
| Market Participant Mix | 80%+ retail, 20% institutional | 30-40% retail, 60-70% institutional | Exploitable retail flow patterns and behaviors           |
| Algorithmic Trading %  | \~15% of volume                | 60-70% of volume                    | Less competition from sophisticated algorithms           |
| Closing Auction        | Yes, 15:00-15:10 AST           | Yes, similar structure              | Specialized auction strategies for liquidity events      |
