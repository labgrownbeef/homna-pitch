# 5.1 Stress Testing

* **Geopolitical Tension Scenario:** Based on regional conflict events and their impact on Saudi markets
* **Oil Price Shock:** Simulating rapid 20%+ changes in oil prices and resulting market volatility
* **Regulatory Change:** Modeling impact of potential Tadawul rule changes on strategy performance
* **Liquidity Freeze:** Testing performance during extreme low-liquidity events
* **Circuit Breaker Event:** Simulating trading during limit up/down scenarios
