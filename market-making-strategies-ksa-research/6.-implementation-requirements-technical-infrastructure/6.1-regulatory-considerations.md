# 6.1 Regulatory Considerations

* **CMA Registration:** Registration with the Capital Market Authority as a market maker
* **Quote Obligations:** Meeting minimum presence requirements in assigned securities
* **Market Manipulation Prevention:** Compliance with anti-manipulation regulations
* **Reporting Requirements:** Daily, weekly and monthly reporting obligations
* **Islamic Finance Compliance:** Ensuring strategies comply with Shariah principles
