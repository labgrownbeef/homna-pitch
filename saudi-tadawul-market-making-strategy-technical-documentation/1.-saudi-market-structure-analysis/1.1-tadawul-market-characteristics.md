# 1.1 Tadawul Market Characteristics

| Parameter                  | Tadawul Value                 | Developed Markets             | Strategic Advantage                          |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Bid-Ask Spread**         | 0.5-3.0%                      | 0.05-0.1%                     | 10-30x wider spread capture opportunity      |
| **Tick Size**              | Tiered (0.01-0.10 SAR)        | Uniform small ticks           | Strategic quote placement at tier boundaries |
| **Trading Hours**          | 10:00-15:00 AST               | 6.5-8 hours                   | Concentrated liquidity periods               |
| **Market Participant Mix** | 80% retail, 20% institutional | 30% retail, 70% institutional | Predictable retail flow patterns             |
| **Algorithmic Trading**    | \~15% of volume               | 60-70% of volume              | Less competition from HFT                    |
| **Cancel-to-Trade Ratio**  | 4:1                           | 10:1                          | More persistent order book                   |
| **Daily Limit**            | ±10%                          | Variable/None                 | Risk management around limits                |
