# Executive Summary

This technical documentation provides a comprehensive implementation guide for HOMNA's market making strategy specifically designed for the Saudi Stock Exchange (Tadawul). The strategy leverages unique Saudi market characteristics including wider bid-ask spreads (0.5-3% vs 0.05-0.1% in developed markets), lower algorithmic competition, and specific intraday patterns driven by prayer times and local market structure.

**Key Performance Metrics:**

* **Expected Annual Return**: 30-85% (57% average)
* **Sharpe Ratio Target**: >3.0 (Currently 3.8)
* **Maximum Drawdown**: <5% (Currently 3.2%)
* **Capital Allocation**: 35% of total trading capital
