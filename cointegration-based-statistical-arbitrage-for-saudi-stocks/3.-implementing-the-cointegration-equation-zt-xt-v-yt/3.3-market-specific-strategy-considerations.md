# 3.3 Market-Specific Strategy Considerations

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-06-08 at 10.03.37 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Our analysis of Saudi equity pairs demonstrates both stronger cointegration relationships and faster mean-reverting properties compared to developed markets. The combined effect of stronger cointegration coefficients and shorter mean-reversion half-lives creates a favorable environment for statistical arbitrage strategies, explaining the substantial outperformance potential in Saudi implementations.

Among all sectors, the banking sector stands out with the strongest cointegration patterns. Pairs combining major Saudi banks like Al Rajhi, NCB, and Alinma demonstrate exceptional statistical properties. The performance of these pairs appears to be enhanced during Ramadan periods, creating seasonal trading opportunities that may be unique to the Saudi market.
