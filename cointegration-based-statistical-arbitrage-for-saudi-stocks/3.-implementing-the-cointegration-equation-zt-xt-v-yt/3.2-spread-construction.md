# 3.2 Spread Construction

Once β is determined, construct the spread Z<sub>t</sub> = X<sub>t</sub> - β·Y<sub>t</sub> and analyze its statistical properties:

* Mean and standard deviation
* Half-life of mean reversion (τ = -log(2)/log(ρ), where ρ is the AR(1) coefficient)
* Stationarity confirmation through ADF test (p-value < 0.05)
