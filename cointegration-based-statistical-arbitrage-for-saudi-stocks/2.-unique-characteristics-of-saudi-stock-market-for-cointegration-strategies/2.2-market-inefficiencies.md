# 2.2 Market Inefficiencies

* Limited algorithmic competition with few sophisticated quantitative players
* [Price distortions due to retail investor dominance (67% of daily trading volume)](https://www.argaam.com/en/article/articledetail/id/1697675)
* Potential information asymmetries between local and international investors
