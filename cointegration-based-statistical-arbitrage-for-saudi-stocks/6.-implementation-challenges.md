# 6. Implementation Challenges

#### 6.1 Saudi-Specific Challenges

* Sudden geopolitical events
* Religious calendar effects ("The Ramadan Effect", Hajj)
* Regulatory changes affecting market structure
* Liquidity constraints during certain periods

#### 6.2 Technical Challenges

* Real-time β estimation and adaptation
* Execution slippage in wider spread environment
* Managing short selling constraints
* Handling trading halts and limit-up/limit-down scenarios
