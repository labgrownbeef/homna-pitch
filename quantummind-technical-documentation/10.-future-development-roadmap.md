# 10. Future Development Roadmap

1. **Multi-Asset Correlation**: Analysis of cross-asset correlations for portfolio recommendations
2. **Adaptive Memory Spans**: Dynamically adjust memory spans based on market volatility
3. **Enhanced Arabic NLP**: Deeper Arabic financial language understanding
4. **Alternative Data Integration**: Satellite imagery, shipping data, and other alternative data sources
5. **Customizable Trading Strategies**: User-defined strategy templates
6. **Advanced Risk Models**: Sophisticated VaR and stress testing capabilities
7. **Interactive Explanations**: Richer interactive explanations of trading recommendations

**Saudi Market Enhancements**

Specific planned improvements for Saudi market coverage:

* Integration with Saudi Central Bank economic data
* Enhanced Tadawul market microstructure analysis
* Saudi-specific event detection (e.g., regulatory changes, Vision 2030 milestones)
* Advanced Arabic dialect processing for social media sentiment
* Cross-GCC market correlation analysis

These enhancements are scheduled for release in quarterly updates over the next 18 months.
